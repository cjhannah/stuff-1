Dogerizer

This script adds an image of Kabosu to the page background, colours all text randomly and makes is Comic Sans and displays bubbles with random words on the page. It also automagically adds jQuery if it isn't present. The bubble words are fetched from the page text while most of the common ones are filtered out to provide the best user experience.
