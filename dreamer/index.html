dreamer

Experimental Mind-/Dreamachine and binaural beats generator. Claims regarding the effects have not been verified, but it's a neat experiment nonetheless.
Headphones required. May be dangerous for people with epilepsy or other nervous disorders.

Instructions

  1. put on headphones
  2. adjust parameters (especially beat frequency) as desired
  3. (optional) play something in the background like music or this
  4. click start and then outside this box to hide it
  5. switch to fullscreen (F11)
  6. (optional) put on a VR device like the Rift or Cardboard
  7. close your eyes
